[i][/i][b]Guede Bousso[/b] is a school from which many noble scholars of the Mouridiyya graduated from , especially the second generation of it, and it was founded by [i]Cheikh Mbacke Bousso[/i] with the permission of [i]Cheikh Al-Khadim[/i], it is a village and a revelation from the neighborhoods of the city of [b]Touba[/b].
[i]Sheikh Mbacke Bousso[/i] founded this village in the twentieth century about (1336 AH, corresponding to 1918 AD)
It is located north of the city of “Touba” two kilometers, and is bordered by "Dar Al-Qudus" to the west, "Touba Soura" to the south, and "Husnul Maab" to the east.

The founder of this village is a scholar who is in a class of his own, unmatched in various types of sciences, from the sciences of Sharia, arithmetic, astronomy and others, and he spent years working in teaching until old age, so Allah granted him a successor in that path. [i]Sheikh Al-Khadim[/i] has assigned him with fatwa, the judiciary and the establishment of islamic borders, and the village of Guede and the [b]Bousobé family[/b] still maintains that to this day.

[b]Guede [/b]is known as the source of science, knowledge and.” [i]The Guedewiya school [/i]“is one of the largest schools in [b]Muridiyya[/b]. Since its creation , it was and still is the center of science, to which students and learners come to receive science, and played a prominent role in broadcasting and spreading science throughout Senegal. The Senegalese researcher, [i]Sheikh Tijan Sy[/i] said: [quote]In the era of Sheikh Ahmadou Bamba, we saw that some of the the searchers were completely cut off for scientific research, and the village of "Guede" did not gain its wide fame except because it is an important scientific center run by the Bousobé family to which the mother of the founder of Mouridia belongs.[/quote]
He added, quoting the famous scientist [i]Sheikh Anta Diop[/i]: [quote]They were interested in mathematics, scientific mechanics and termo- dynamics - especially time measurement, and the latter was related to the need to establish prayer on time for it.[/quote]
